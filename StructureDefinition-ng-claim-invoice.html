Resource Profile: NG-Claim Invoice

Official URL: https://dhin.org/2025Connectathon/StructureDefinition/ng-claim-invoice Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2025-08-02 Computable Name: NgClaimInvoice

A profile for invoices generated by providers for reimbursement.

Usages:

  • Use this Profile: 1-NG-Bundle: Hospital to HMO (Claim Submission) and 3-NG-Bundle: Patient to Hospital
  • Examples for this Profile: Invoice/invoice-001 and Invoice/invoice-003
